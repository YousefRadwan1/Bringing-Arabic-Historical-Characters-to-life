About Our Project

Revolutionizing education through interactive historical experiences

Our Vision

This is our graduation project - a revolutionary educational platform designed to transform how students learn about historical characters. We are a dedicated team of 6 students who recognized the challenges in traditional history education and decided to create an innovative solution.

Our platform makes learning interactive and memorable by allowing students to have real conversations with historical figures. Instead of reading endless textbooks, students can now ask questions directly to characters like Salah El-Din Al-Ayyubi and Mohamed Ali Pasha, getting personalized answers and filling knowledge gaps in an engaging way.

Interactive Learning

Students can engage in real-time conversations with historical characters, making learning more engaging and memorable.

Fill Knowledge Gaps

Students can ask specific questions about missing information, getting personalized answers tailored to their learning needs.

Enhanced Retention

Interactive conversations create stronger memory connections, helping students remember historical facts and stories better.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize educational platforms by making historical learning more accessible, interactive, and engaging. We believe that every student deserves to experience history in a way that sparks curiosity and deepens understanding.

Through cutting-edge AI technology and 3D avatars, we're bridging the gap between past and present, allowing students to learn directly from the great minds of history. Our goal is to make education more effective by transforming passive reading into active dialogue.
